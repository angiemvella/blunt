ScopeShare in a Nutshell

ScopeShare gives you live realtime access to a network of telescopes while also bringing you a brand new way to use, share and monetise your own telescope — a platform where anyone around the world can watch, control and use your telescope LIVE in realtime for Astronomical Photography, Research, Education or just for Fun!

Observers can instantly watch any realtime live scope feed from any scope on the network currently online, take high resolution snapshots and even chat to the telescope owner when available, for FREE!

Observers can also buy ‘Scope Minutes’, blocks of control time, allowing them to take full remote robotic control of any of the available telescopes for observation of any region of space visible to any scope they choose.

Owners can monetize their telescopes with the ScopeShare hardware kit (a wifi enabled embedded device, with it's own camera module, focus adaptor and dataport for movement control) and receive proceeds of the Scope Minutes which are split with the telescope owner, generating an income for any ScopeShare enabled telescope owner.
